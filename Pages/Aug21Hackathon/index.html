Mintbean Hackathon August 2021


Welcome! So this was something totally new and unfamiliar that I did. I had never done a hackathon before but when I heard about this one and how it was apparently a pretty good intro to hackathons, so decided to jump in.

With a prompt for a 2D game, I had a million ideas with no idea where to begin. I ended up not being able to figure out characters and movement fast enough, so I rolled with a short and simple trivia game. This project gave me the opportunity to learn and use Zim.js which was brand new to me, but I found it was super interesting and I thoroughly enjoyed working with it.

Even though I wasn't able to get my initial ideas into any sort of functional shape, it was a great experience and I met some awesome people, some who I still talk to now months later. It was definitely something I will always remember and I am glad I took that step and jumped into a hackathon.
