Mintbean Hackathon August 2021

Hey all! So this is something totally new and unfamiliar that I did. I had never done a hackathon before but when I heard about this one and how it was apparently a pretty chill and relaxed hackathon, I thought, why not?? I had been wanting to try doing on for quite a while, but thought they were all very much out of my reach. I was super excited when I heard the prompt was building a game and I had SO many ideas. What I did NOT realize was how confusing it would be to try and learn about sprites and character movement and level building in a short timeframe, especially as someone with pretty limited knowledge on web development and frontend work.


I ended up not being able to figure these things out in a reasonable time frame and ended up just rolling with a trivia game of sorts. It is definitely a much plainer project than I had originally planned to do, but I had the opportunity to learn a ton about Zimjs which was compltely unfamiliar to me and found I thoroughly enjoy it! It is definitely a really cool framework and includes some amazing examples and tools to be used. I absolutely think this will be something I will be coming back to and using again.


So this experience was definitely not what I had planned on day 1, but it still ended up being an awesome experience and I plan to continue working on this project, probably adding more questions and working in some form of save method to save the level that a user is on. I am super grateful to have had this opportunity to not only continue working on learning Javascript/html/css but also bringing in a framework that I had never even heard of and become familiar with it as well.
